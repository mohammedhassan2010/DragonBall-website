Dragon Ball

Dragon Ball is a Japanese media franchise created by Akira Toriyama in 1984. It has five anime installments based on the franchise that have been produced by Toei Animation: Dragon Ball (1986); Dragon Ball Z (1989); Dragon Ball GT (1996); and Dragon Ball Super(2015); followed by the web series Super Dragon Ball Heroes (2018).
